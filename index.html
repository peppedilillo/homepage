Work

I am an astrophysicist and experimental physicist with an interest for computer science and applied math. Currently, I work for the Italian National Institute for Astrophysics, at the Italian Space Agency Space Science Data Centre. There, I mainly deal with data and software supporting high-energy space missions. In the past I spent a lot of my time in labs, where I worked with high-energy, solid-state detectors and nanosatellites.

A complete list of my articles is available on NASA/ADS or Research Gate.
